Spicy Asian Ramen Noodles

Description

Spicy Asian ramen noodles are a delicious and popular dish that originated in Japan. Made with a combination of noodles, broth, and a variety of spices, these noodles are the perfect way to add some heat to your meal.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles (2 packs)
  • Chicken or vegetable broth (4 cups)
  • Soy sauce (2 tablespoons)
  • Sesame oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Ginger (1 tablespoon, grated)
  • Sriracha sauce (1 tablespoon)
  • Red pepper flakes (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Scallions (2, sliced)
  • Carrots (1, peeled and julienned)
  • Bean sprouts (1 cup)
  • Hard-boiled eggs (2, sliced)
  • Cilantro (1/4 cup, chopped)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate pot, heat the chicken or vegetable broth over medium heat.
  3. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, Sriracha sauce, and red pepper flakes to the broth and stir to combine.
  4. Add the scallions, carrots, and bean sprouts to the broth and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Divide the cooked ramen noodles between 2-3 bowls and ladle the broth and vegetables over the noodles.
  6. Garnish each bowl with sliced hard-boiled eggs and chopped cilantro.
