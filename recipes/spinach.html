Spinach, Egg, Pancetta with Linguine

Description

Linguine with spinach, egg, and pancetta is a flavorful dish that combines pasta with the richness of egg and the saltiness of pancetta.

Ingredients

  • Linguine pasta - 1 pound
  • Pancetta - 6 ounces (diced)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Spinach - 6 ounces (roughly chopped)
  • Eggs - 2 large
  • Heavy cream - 1/2 cup
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Red pepper flakes - optional, for spice

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the linguine and cook until al dente, according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining the linguine.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy, stirring occasionally. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the roughly chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together 2 eggs, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add the cooked linguine and reserved pasta water to the skillet with the spinach and pancetta. Toss everything together to combine.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and pour the egg and cream mixture over the linguine. Toss everything together quickly, using tongs, until the sauce thickens and coats the linguine. Be careful not to scramble the eggs.
  7. Remove the skillet from heat and sprinkle red pepper flakes, if using, over the linguine. Toss everything together one more time.
  8. Divide the linguine among bowls and serve hot. Enjoy!
